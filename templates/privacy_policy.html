How We Use Your Information

Your data is used strictly to operate and improve the platform. We do not use it for advertising or profiling outside of {{ org_name }}.

  • Authenticate your account and maintain session security
  • Process exam submissions and generate detailed performance reports
  • Provide topic-level analytics and progress tracking over time
  • Deliver AI-generated study assistance through the built-in chat assistant
  • Notify you about exam schedules, results, and important account activity
  • Investigate and resolve technical issues or support requests
  • Detect and prevent exam fraud, account misuse, or security incidents
  • Improve question generation models and platform features
We do not use your data to train external AI models, serve advertisements, or share insights with marketing partners.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Sharing only occurs in the following limited and necessary circumstances:

  • Your institution: Exam results and session logs may be shared with the educational institution or administrator who created the exam
  • Service providers: Infrastructure partners (cloud hosting, database, file storage) operate under strict confidentiality agreements and process data only on our behalf
  • AI services: Exam content and study queries are processed by third-party AI APIs (Google Gemini, Groq) for question generation and study assistance — no personally identifiable information is included in these requests
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data when required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of users
Exam questions and responses shared with AI services are stripped of user identifiers before processing. We do not send your name, email, or account details to any AI provider.
4

Security

We apply industry-standard security practices to protect your data at rest and in transit:

  • All passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plaintext passwords
  • HTTPS enforced across the entire platform — no unencrypted data transmission
  • Database access is restricted to application-level service accounts with least-privilege roles
  • Redis session store is isolated and not publicly accessible
  • Rate limiting on login endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks
  • Single-device session enforcement — logging in on a new device invalidates previous sessions

While we take every reasonable precaution, no system is entirely immune to risk. If you suspect your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and contact us at {{ org_email }}.
