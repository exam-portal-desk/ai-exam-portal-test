How it works

From setup to results
in minutes

A straightforward workflow for admins and a distraction-free experience for students.

1
Admin creates exam
Set title, date, duration, marking scheme, and add questions from the bank or via CSV upload.
2
Students register
Students create an account, receive a setup email, and access their dashboard with assigned exams.
3
Exam is attempted
Timed, locked session. MCQ, MSQ or numerical answers. Auto-submit on time expiry. One device at a time.
4
Results & review
Instant score, grade, and detailed response page. Discuss each question. Admin views all analytics.
