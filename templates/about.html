Our Mission

To give every educational institution — from a single-teacher coaching centre to a university — the same quality of exam infrastructure that was previously available only to large organisations. No bloated setup, no per-seat enterprise contracts, no compromise on security.

Platform Capabilities

Exam Security

Fullscreen lockdown, tab-switch detection, and keyboard shortcut blocking keep each session honest.

AI Question Generation

Gemini-powered generator creates MCQ and MSQ questions from uploaded PDFs and study material.

Performance Analytics

Per-question accuracy, topic-level breakdown, and trend graphs available immediately after submission.

AI Study Assistant

Built-in chat powered by LLaMA 3.3 70B helps students clear doubts between exam attempts.

Live Exam Monitoring

Real-time timer, auto-submit on timeout, and focus-loss alerts give admins full visibility.

Flexible Question Bank

Upload questions via CSV or generate them with AI. Supports LaTeX, images, and formula rendering.

Built With

SmartAIExam is a Flask/Jinja2 web application backed by PostgreSQL (Supabase), Redis for sessions, and Google Drive for media storage — optimised for reliability and cost-efficiency.
